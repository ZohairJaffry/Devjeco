About us :

DevJeco is an AI & ML company that aims to bring revolution to the information technology sector. We are currently looking for talented Graphic Designer to join our team.

Responsibilities :

  • • Create visually appealing and engaging graphics for various digital media, including website design, social media content, marketing collateral, presentations, and more.
  • • Collaborate closely with our marketing, development, and design teams to translate concepts and ideas into captivating visual designs.
  • • Produce design elements that align with our brand identity, maintaining consistency and high-quality standards across all projects.
  • • Stay up-to-date with industry trends, design techniques, and emerging technologies to ensure our designs remain fresh and innovative.
  • • Contribute to brainstorming sessions, providing creative input and innovative design ideas.
  • • Manage multiple projects simultaneously, ensuring deadlines are met and deliverables are of the highest quality.

Qualifications :

  • • Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Visual Arts, or related field (or equivalent work experience).
  • • Proven experience as a Graphic Designer, showcasing a strong portfolio of diverse design projects.
  • • Proficiency in industry-standard design software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Adobe Creative Suite etc.).
  • • Solid understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and layout techniques.
  • • Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively within a multidisciplinary team.
  • • Detail-oriented and capable of producing accurate work in a fast-paced environment.
  • • Ability to take creative direction and incorporate feedback into design iterations.
  • • Familiarity with UI/UX design principles and responsive design is a plus.
  • • The candidate must have at-least 2 years of experience.
