DevJeco is a leading IT and AI solutions provider dedicated to revolutionizing industries through cutting-edge technologies. We specialize in technologies, e.g., machine learning, cloud computing, etc. and have a proven track record of delivering innovative solutions to clients worldwide.

DevJeco is expanding into Dubai and we are currently assembling a team to implement AI solutions. We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Business Development Executive to join our team. The ideal candidate will play a pivotal role in driving business growth, forging strategic partnerships, and expanding our client base.

Key Responsibilities :

  • • Identify and pursue new business opportunities to achieve sales targets and revenue goals.
  • • Leverage Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce, and LinkedIn Sales Navigator to identify and pursue new business opportunities, ensuring sales targets and revenue goals are met.

  • • Keep abreast of industry developments and advancements in IT and AI technologies.
