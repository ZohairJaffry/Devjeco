Why Choose Hack The Future?

Event Overview

Our program will have a specific focus on AI projects, allowing students to delve into the exciting realm of AI. We have designed the program to accommodate their needs. To ensure maximum interest, we will organize the program over two days. The participants will be divided into 12 groups, with each group consisting of four individuals, fostering teamwork, and a supportive learning space.

Impact On Candidates

The Hack the Future series will emphasize project-based learning, where students actively apply their newly acquired skills to real-world scenarios. By working on AI projects, students will gain practical experience in problem identification, ideation, prototyping, and implementation. This hands-on approach enables them to develop a deeper understanding of AI concepts and technologies.

Future Implications

We recognize the importance of networking and building connections in today's interconnected world. The Hack the Future series will provide ample networking opportunities for students to connect with like-minded peers, mentors, and professionals. These connections can lead to collaborations, future career prospects, and a sense of belonging within the AI community.


Teams

Rules and Regulations


Eligibility

  • The competition is open to individuals or teams of individuals
  • Participants must be at least 18 years old or of legal age in their respective countries.

    • Registration

  • Participants must register online through the designated registration portal.
  • 12 team members must be registered before the specified deadline.
  • Each participant must provide accurate and complete information during registration.

    • Team Formation

  • Teams can consist of a minimum of four members.
  • Participants may only be a member of one team.
  • Team composition cannot be changed after the registration deadline.

    • Code of Conduct

  • Participants must adhere to a high standard of behavior and treat others with respect and professionalism.
  • Discrimination, harassment, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Participants must follow the event organizer's instructions and guidelines throughout the event.
  • Any violation of the code of conduct may result in immediate disqualification.

    • Project Requirements

  • Projects must be original and developed during the event.
  • Participants must use the designated programming languages, platforms, or frameworks specified by the organizers.
  • The project should align with the specified theme or problem statement provided by the organizers.
