Welcome to the DevJeco MTO Program

At DevJeco, we believe in nurturing exceptional talent and empowering the next generation of business and technology leaders. Our Management Trainee Officer (MTO) Program is designed to provide aspiring professionals with a unique opportunity to fast-track their careers, gain hands-on experience, and unlock their full potential


Why Choose the DevJeco MTO Program?

Accelerate Your Growth

The MTO Program is a transformative journey that propels your professional development. Through a comprehensive curriculum, mentoring, and challenging assignments, you'll acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the dynamic fields of business development and project management.

Immersive Learning Experience

Immerse yourself in a collaborative and innovative environment where you'll work alongside industry experts. Gain practical insights, explore emerging technologies, and tackle real-world challenges to sharpen your problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills.

Rotational Exposure

As an MTO, you'll have the opportunity to experience different facets of DevJeco's operations. Through rotations in business development, marketing, and project management, you'll develop a well-rounded skill set, expand your horizons, and gain a holistic understanding of our organization.

Mentorship and Guidance

We believe in the power of mentorship. Throughout the program, you'll receive guidance from seasoned professionals who will support your growth, provide valuable insights, and help you navigate your career path within DevJeco.

Impactful Projects

Make a tangible impact through your contributions to high-impact projects. Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams, drive innovation, and witness the transformative power of AI as we revolutionize industries and shape the future.

What Streams Are Available?

  • Software Development
  • HR & Operations
  • Business Development
  • Technical Project Management
  • Data Science

Hiring Process

How To Get Started

01

Initial Application Round

Candidates complete an application form which shall act as the initial screening phase. Candidates who succeed at this stage shall be invited to complete an online screening test which shall include a personality test, and a task to demonstrate abilities and commitment to excellence. We review these applications thoroughly to shortlist the most promising candidates.

02

Assessment Center

Shortlisted candidates are invited to the assessment center, where they participate in a series of activities and interviews to showcase their skills, potential, and alignment with DevJeco's values and vision

03

Final Evaluation

Based on the assessments from , we conduct a thorough evaluation of each candidate's performance, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of their capabilities and fit within our organization.

04

Offer Extension

The most exceptional candidates who successfully complete the evaluation process will receive an offer to join DevJeco. Our offers reflect our commitment to market-competitive compensation and the opportunity for growth and advancement. Once candidates accept our offer, we provide a comprehensive onboarding process to ensure a smooth transition into their roles at DevJeco. We believe in supporting the growth and success of our employees from day one.
