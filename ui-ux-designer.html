Company Overview :

DevJeco is a leading provider of innovative software solutions, specializing in AI, ML & Big Data. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality products to our diverse client base, and we are seeking a skilled Technical Project Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will play a crucial role in managing stakeholders, coordinating project activities, and ensuring the successful execution of our software development projects.

Job Summary :

We are seeking a talented and creative UI/UX Figma Designer to join our software development team. The ideal candidate is passionate about user-centric design and has a strong background in creating visually appealing and interactive prototypes using Figma. As a UI/UX Figma Designer, you will collaborate closely with our clients and development team to translate user requirements into stunning design concepts and intuitive user experiences.

Responsibilities :

  • • Collaborate with clients and internal stakeholders to understand project requirements, user needs, and business goals.
  • • Create high-quality user interfaces and engaging user experiences using Figma.
  • • Design and iterate on wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes that effectively communicate design concepts and user flows.
  • • Conduct user research and gather feedback to inform design decisions and validate design solutions.
  • • Collaborate with front-end developers to ensure seamless integration of design and functionality.
  • • Stay up to date with the latest UI/UX trends, techniques, and technologies to continuously improve design processes and deliver innovative solutions.
  • • Participate in design reviews and provide constructive feedback to fellow designers. Maintain design consistency and ensure adherence to brand guidelines across multiple projects.
  • • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers, developers, and QA engineers, to deliver high-quality design deliverables within project timelines.

Qualifications :

  • • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing your UI/UX design skills and Figma proficiency.
  • • Expertise in designing visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • • Proficiency in using Figma or similar design tools to create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes.
  • • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles, information architecture, and usability testing methodologies.
  • • Ability to translate complex user requirements into intuitive and seamless user experiences.
  • • Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to effectively present and justify design decisions to clients and team members.
  • • Knowledge of front-end development technologies (HTML, CSS, etc.) and their impact on design implementation is a plus.
  • • Attention to detail, with a focus on delivering pixel-perfect designs and maintaining design consistency.
  • • Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
  • • Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field is preferred.
